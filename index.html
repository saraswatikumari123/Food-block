Ras Malai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 saffron strands
  • 1/2 cup paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil milk in a pan and simmer for 10 minutes. Add sugar, saffron, and cardamom powder, and stir until sugar dissolves. Let it cool.
  2. Knead paneer until smooth. Make small, flat round balls.
  3. Add the paneer balls into the prepared syrup and refrigerate for 2-3 hours.
  4. Garnish with pistachios and almonds before serving.

Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil soaked chickpeas with salt until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds, and saut√© onions till golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and spices. Cook till oil separates.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer for 10 minutes. Garnish with coriander.

Chocolate Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix condensed milk, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture.
  4. Add chocolate chips if desired.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and freeze for 6-8 hours.
